FAQs

Find answers to your most pressing questions about our hotel and services.

What are the check-in times?

Check-in is available from 3 PM onwards. If you arrive earlier, we can store your luggage. Please inform us in advance if you need special arrangements.

Is breakfast included?

Yes, a complimentary breakfast is included with your stay. It features a variety of options to suit all tastes. Enjoy it in our dining area from 7 AM to 10 AM.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations can be made up to 48 hours before your arrival without penalty. After that, a fee may apply. Please check your reservation details for specific terms.

Do you allow pets?

Yes, we welcome pets in designated rooms. A small fee applies for pet stays. Please inform us at the time of booking to ensure availability.

How can I contact you?

You can reach us via phone or email. Our contact information is available on our website. We're here to assist you with any inquiries.
